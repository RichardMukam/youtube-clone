Nested Layouts
You can recreate a website by breaking down all of the elements
into vertical/horizontal elements.
Divs by default have a width of 100%
Images overflow in HTML + CSS, by default they overflow, even
their divs. They keep their original dimensions regardless of
the requirements with the div.

Back-up font format/syntax.
It's formally called a font stack.
If Roboto cannot load, the text on the page will load with Arial.
font-family: Roboto, Arial;
